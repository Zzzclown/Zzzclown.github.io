Xcode Alternatives
Xcode is described as ‘is Apple’s premiere development environment for Mac OS X’ and is a very popular IDE in the Development category. There are more than 50 alternatives to Xcode for a variety of platforms, including Windows, Mac, Linux, BSD and Online / Web-based. The best alternative is Sublime Text. It’s not free, so if you’re looking for a free alternative, you could try Eclipse or IntelliJ IDEA. Other great apps like Xcode are Microsoft Visual Studio (Free Personal), Apache NetBeans (Free, Open Source), Code::Blocks (Free, Open Source) and Qt Creator (Free, Open Source).
