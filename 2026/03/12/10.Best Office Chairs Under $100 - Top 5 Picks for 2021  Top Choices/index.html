Best Office Chairs Under $100 – Top 5 Picks for 2021
by Rhoda Jane Pickens

5 Highest Reviewed Office Chairs that are Under $100
For people who sit at a desk for hours every day, there is no more important piece of furniture than their office chair. Doctors report that uncomfortable or poorly designed office chairs can lead to back problems and headaches, among other discomforts. Having the right office chair can make a difference to the way workers do their jobs and to the quality of their work. One study found that office workers who had quality seating saw an increase in their productivity of 17.5%.
Just as each individual’s body is unique, however, each people has different needs for their office chair. There are numerous factors to take into account when choosing the best office chair for oneself or one’s employees. Key factors in that choice include the chairs’ appearance, their size, and the level of each chair’s adjustability. And of course, the cost is always a factor when purchasing furniture.
While there are many overpriced pieces on the market, it’s absolutely possible to find a quality office chair for less than 100 dollars. That’s because advanced technology has brought down the price of office furniture, making comfort and style available to everyone. To make finding the best office chair under 100 dollars easier, here are the five of best options on the market today.
1.) BestOffice Ergonomic PU Leather High Back Office Chair
For total body support at a reasonable price, there is the BestOffice Ergonomic High Back Office Chair. This chair works well for people of all sizes, including taller individuals, up to 250 lbs. It boasts thick padding on both the seat and up its tall back.
The chair offers full 360-degree rotation and easy tilt-back reclining. Buyers report that it is easy and quick to assemble, which is especially helpful for people purchasing a chair for their home office. Made of strong PU leather, this chair is a conservative black with matte silver arms and legs and looks modern and professional.
PROS
This office chair is easy and quick to assemble.
This chair supports taller and heavier people.
Reclining this chair is easy and moves smoothly.
CONS
This chair may not provide adequate lumbar support for extended sitting.

2.) AmazonBasics Mid-Back Office Chair
For a simple, classic chair at a surprisingly low price, consider the AmazonBasics Mid-Back Office Chair. This affordable option provides some back support and cushioning, an improvement over unpadded chairs.
The black bonded leather and black plastic give this chair a minimalist, uncluttered appearance. This chair only supports people up to 225 lbs., which is probably not enough to reliably fit every office worker. The seat has pneumatic height adjustment to fit a variety of heights, although it is more comfortable for smaller individuals. It sits on dual casters and should roll easily on carpeted floors.

3.) Essentials Leather Executive Computer/Office Chair with Arms
This is possibly the best office chair under 100 dollars designed specifically for lower back support. Both the height of the seat and the seat tilt are fully adjustable, making it a good fit for people of all heights. The weight limit is 250 pounds, and thick padding on the seat means that this chair is comfortable for larger customers as well.
This office chair is made of a smooth black material called SofThread Leather, which is comfortable but may not be as long lasting as other materials. While the seat and padding on the armrests are black, the legs and arms themselves are a matte silver, giving this chair an attractive and modern look.

4.) Office Star Faux Leather Manager’s Chair with Padded Arms
A large, plush headrest sets this chair apart from the pack. It’s made of a soft faux leather in a solid black color with contrasting white stitching. The back of this chair provides lumbar support and a thick cushion for back comfort.
However, the seat is less cushioned and may not offer enough comfort for larger individuals. The bottom of the chair is made of a strong nylon, and it rolls smoothly on dual wheel casters, even on carpet.
This office chair works well for shorter and smaller individuals since the adjustable chair height ranges from 16 to 19 inches from the floor. Padded arms and a locking tilt control add extra comfort.

This attractive chair covers all the basics of comfort, starting with soft, PU leather upholstery. PU leather is leather that has been covered polyurethane for strength and durability. This chair also provides 360-degree swiveling, and the height is adjustable between 19 and 23 inches.
The most appealing element of this chair, however, may be its striking brown color. Although black matches every decor, brown is a more rich color, imparting an air of old-fashioned luxury. This office chair fits people up to 250 lbs, and a heavy metal base keeps it grounded for safety and security.

Wrapping Up
Having the right chair can make any office worker happier and more productive. Buyers should take into account the height of the individuals who will be using the seat, as well as their personal needs for back and head support.
And, of course, they should consider the aesthetics of the office in which the chair will be placed. Luckily, there are a variety of options, all under 100 dollars, proving that you don’t need to spend a lot of money to sit in style and comfort.
